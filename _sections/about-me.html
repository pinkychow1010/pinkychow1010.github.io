Hello! I am Ka Hei, a young geoscientist from Hong Kong specializing in geospatial data, earth observation and machine learning. I am a mountain enthusiast and a global citizen interested in sustainability and AI. My research interests include climate risk, resilience and disaster management. My data science experience includes building machine learning models using EO data and developing geospatial data products for scientific research communities and business audiences. I have cooperated with NGOs and research institutions on remote sensing applications, including the Global SnowPack project from the German Aerospace Centre (DLR). Currently, I am a climate risk consultant working for the Poverty Reduction and Equity Group of the World Bank. In my free time, I like to learn about culture, cognitive psychology and evolution.
